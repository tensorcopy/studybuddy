# Research Brief Template

Use this exact structure for all research briefs:

```markdown
---
topic: "<Topic Name>"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
status: research-brief
---

# Research Brief: <Topic Name>

## Summary
2-3 paragraph overview: what it is, why it matters, where the field stands now.

## Key Concepts
Ordered from foundational to advanced. For each:
- **Concept name**: 2-3 sentence explanation
- Why it matters
- Common misconceptions

## Important Papers & Sources
For each key source:
- **[Title](url)** by Authors (Year)
  - Key contribution
  - Relevant findings
  - How it connects to our topic

## Technical Details
Core equations, algorithms, or architectures. Include LaTeX ($...$ inline, $$...$$ display).

## Code Opportunities
Where runnable examples would add value. Include pseudocode sketches.

## Open Questions
What's unresolved, debated, or cutting-edge.

## Suggested Post Structure
Recommended H2 outline for a blog post.

## Raw Notes
Additional context, quotes, data points worth keeping.
```

## Quality bar

- Brief should be 2,000+ words
- At least 10 papers/sources cited with URLs
- Key equations included in LaTeX
- At least 3 code opportunities identified
- Concepts ordered by prerequisite chain
